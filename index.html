Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What is the check-in/check-out time? +

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transfers? +

Yes, we offer complimentary airport transfers for all luxury suite bookings. Standard transfers can be arranged at an additional cost.

Is breakfast included? +

Yes, complimentary breakfast is included with all room bookings. Our breakfast features a wide variety of international and local options.

Can I cancel my booking? +

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellation policies may vary for special offers.

Do you have parking facilities? +

Yes, secure parking is available at all our properties. Valet parking service is complementary for all room types.

Are pets allowed? +

We welcome well-behaved pets in designated pet-friendly rooms. A nightly pet fee applies.
